Join our events and become a part of our community

At Paws & Claws Emporium, we believe in bringing pet lovers together. That's why we host a variety of events throughout the year to help you connect with other pet owners and learn more about how to care for your furry friends.

Upcoming Events

  • Pet Adoption Day

    Join us on Saturday, March 12th from 12pm to 4pm for our annual Pet Adoption Day. We'll have dogs, cats, and other furry friends available for adoption, as well as food, drinks, and fun activities for the whole family.

    Location: Paws & Claws Emporium parking lot

  • Pet Health Seminar

    Join us on Wednesday, April 5th at 6pm for our Pet Health Seminar. Our guest speaker, Dr. Jane Smith, will be discussing the importance of regular check-ups, vaccinations, and preventative care for your pets.

    Location: Paws & Claws Emporium conference room

  • Pet Costume Contest

    Join us on Saturday, October 29th from 2pm to 4pm for our annual Pet Costume Contest. Dress up your pet in their best Halloween costume and compete for a chance to win prizes and bragging rights.

    Location: Paws & Claws Emporium courtyard

Our Community

At Paws & Claws Emporium, we're more than just a pet store. We're a community of pet lovers who share a passion for animal welfare and education. Follow us on social media to stay up-to-date on ongoing events, pet care tips, and cute animal photos.
